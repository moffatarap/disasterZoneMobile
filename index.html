Disaster Kit:

In the event of a disaster having a disaster kit will greatly help you manage to support yourself and family. Below are some recommended items for your own personal disaster kit. Your kit should have the supplies needed for up-to three days.

Survival Items:

  • Battery powered torch, with spare battery's or self-charging.
  • Portable radio
  • First aid kit and any prescription meds
  • Blankets and sleeping bags
  • Pet supply's
  • Toilet paper and rubbish bags
  • Lighter or matches
  • Cooking gas

Food and Water:

  • Canned food
  • Dried food
  • Water three liters per person per day
  • Water for washing and cooking
  • Portable cooker gas powered
  • Can opener

Store all items in a cool dark place, and check the supplies every 12 months.
